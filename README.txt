===============
Django Countria
===============

A full collection of countries, cities, currency, continents and states for Django.

Infomation like calling code, top level domain, latitude, longtitude, population, capital city, etc.

- Approximately 270 Countries
- Approximately 190,000 Cities
- Approximately 160 Currencies
- Approximately 4200 States
